<h2>How HanDeDict @ Zydeo uses cookies</h2>

<p>A cookie is a small piece of text sent to your browser by a website you visit.
It helps the website to remember information about your preferences, such as the
display language or your search options. This website does not include any information
in cookies that can be used to identify visitors, and it does not collect any personal
information through cookies.</p>

<p>You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept
cookies, but you can modify your browser’s settings to decline them. Doing so will
reduce your experience of HanDeDict @ Zydeo.</p>
